General Pricing

Honesty and Transparency

At Eglinton and Yonge Computers, we believe in transparency and honesty. I provide high-quality services in exchange for fair compensation, with no hidden fees. You'll receive a full breakdown of costs before any work begins, and payment is made upfront to ensure everything is clear from the start. The general pricing for builds is 10% plus a flat fee(applied to computers only, not incuding shipping), which varies depending on category.

Shipping and Remote Work

Based near Yonge & Eglinton, all services at Eglinton and Yonge Computers are completed in-house. On-site repairs and delivery within the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) are available for an additional fee. Currently, services are not offered outside the GTA, though special arrangements can be made on request.
