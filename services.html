General Pricing

Honesty and Transparency

My pricing is transparent and honest. I don't hide any fees, and will tell you the complete cost before anything happens, and payment is made upfront. The general pricing for builds is 10% plus a flat fee(applied to computers only, not incuding shipping), which varies depending on category.

Shipping and Remote Work

Eglinton and Yonge Computers, is as the name suggests, based near Yonge & Eglinton. All services are done in house, but you can purchase on-site repairs and delivery seperately. I currently don't provide work outside of the GTA, but room for negotiation is available.
