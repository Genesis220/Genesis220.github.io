Why Us?

My name is Nathan Lau, and I run Eglinton & Yonge Computers. You can see my portfolio here, or in the menu. I believe that a computer should be fast, reliable, and intuitive, while still offering endless possibilities for mastery. I’ve been building computers since I was 13 and have worked with almost every operating system and computer format.

At Eglinton & Yonge Computers, I don’t just build you a computer; I make sure you’re confident in using it. I’ll guide you through basic troubleshooting, teach you how to recognize and handle viruses, and ensure you can make the most of your new system. If you’re interested, I can even help set you up with a customizable Linux OS and show you how to take full advantage of your computer's potential.

My custom-built computers are priced similarly to many prebuilt models, but with me, you’re not just buying a machine. You’re also getting the knowledge and support needed to maintain, fix,
and upgrade your system in the future. I believe that owning a computer means being able to grow with it,
and I’m here to make sure you can do just that. To get started, see the button on the right.Services
