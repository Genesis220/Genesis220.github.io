Why Choose Eglinton and Yonge Computers?

My name is Nathan Lau, and I run Eglinton & Yonge Computers. You can view my portfolio here or via the menu. I believe a computer should be fast, reliable, and intuitive, while offering endless possibilities for customization and mastery. I’ve been building computers since I was 13 and have extensive experience with various operating systems and computer formats.

At Eglinton & Yonge Computers, I don’t just build computers—I ensure you’re confident in using them. I’ll guide you through basic troubleshooting, teach you how to recognize and handle viruses, and help you make the most of your new system. If you’re interested, I can even set you up with a customizable Linux OS, and show you how to unlock the full potential of your computer.

My custom-built computers are competitively priced with prebuilt models, but offer much more value. You’re not just buying a machine; you’re gaining knowledge and support to maintain, upgrade, and grow with your system. Ready to get started?
