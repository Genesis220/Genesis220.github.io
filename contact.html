Contact Us

If you'd like to request a custom build or any modifications/upgrades, please fill out the form below and provide the necessary details about your requirements.

Guidelines for Build Requests

Please include the following information to help us understand your PC needs:

  • Price range: How much are you willing to spend on this PC?
  • Purpose: What will the PC primarily be used for? (e.g., gaming, video editing, programming, etc.)
  • Preferred Components: Any specific components you want? (e.g., processor, graphics card, RAM)
  • Performance Requirements: Any performance standards or benchmarks you're aiming for?
  • Other Features: Any other features you'd like? (e.g., cooling system, RGB lighting, overclocking capabilities)
  • If you don't know any of this info, don't worry! I can arrange a meeting and we can talk about what type of computer you are looking for.

Guidelines for PC Modifications/Upgrades

For upgrade or modification requests, please include:

  • Current PC Specs: What components are currently installed in your system?
  • Upgrade Goals: What are you hoping to improve? (e.g., performance, graphics, storage)
  • Budget: How much are you willing to spend on upgrades?
  • Again, don't stress the details, we can figure it out later!
